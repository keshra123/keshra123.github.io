Our Mission

The Service Buddy is a website to help organizations, individuals, and companies/institutions connect through the need for service animals by indiduals affected by health-related problems and/or disabilites.

In our website, we have provided a variety of different tabs that provide different information. For finding service training locations, we have place to locate service animal training facilities across the globe. We also have an area for service animal trainers to submit notes or mistakes in training directly on site as well as area for signing up for volunteering, donations, and listing animals that are eligible for training and/or need resources. Finally, we provide information about the website, testomonials, and contact information, and more.
